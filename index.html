My Story

Accessibility isn’t a checklist. It’s a commitment to people.

I’m Lidia Ochoa, a UX designer and accessibility specialist for educational platforms. What makes my approach different is that my 7 year career as an educator working directly with students with disabilities allows me to evaluate beyond compliance checklists. I’ve watched talented learners struggle with platforms that weren’t built with them in mind.

That experience has transformed how I see design. Accessibility isn’t an afterthought or a compliance checkbox — it’s a fundamental part of whether your platform actually works for the people it’s supposed to serve.

I hold a Google UX Certificate and specialize in evaluating EdTech platforms through the lens of real usability: not just what the code says, but what the learner actually experiences.
